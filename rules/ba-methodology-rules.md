# BA Methodology Rules

BA-kit supports hybrid delivery by default, with support for Agile and traditional BA methods.

Related rules:
- [BA Primary Workflow](./ba-primary-workflow.md)
- [BA Quality Standards](./ba-quality-standards.md)

## Framework
- Use BABOK 3.0 knowledge areas as the reference baseline.
- Choose the lightest method that still satisfies the engagement.
- Adapt deliverables to the delivery style, not the other way around.

## Agile BA
- Favor user stories, lightweight specs, and rapid validation.
- Keep acceptance criteria explicit and testable.
- Use story mapping, backlog refinement, and iterative change control.

## Traditional BA
- Use BRD, FRD, and SRS structure when formal documentation is needed.
- Capture approvals, assumptions, dependencies, and change control clearly.
- Keep scope, traceability, and sign-off visible.

## Hybrid BA
- Combine formal analysis where risk is high and lightweight artifacts where speed matters.
- Match artifact depth to business criticality, regulatory exposure, and audience.

## Practice Rules
- Start with discovery before solutioning.
- Validate requirements against business goals before finalizing.
- Always consider stakeholder impact and compliance context.
- Prefer evidence over assumption and structure over prose drift.
