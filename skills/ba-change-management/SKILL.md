# BA Change Management

Use this skill when a business or process change will affect people, roles, or habits.

## Workflow
1. Define the change and affected groups.
2. Assess impact on process, people, systems, and controls.
3. Gauge readiness and resistance risks.
4. Identify training and communication needs.
5. Define adoption measures and support actions.
6. Capture rollback and contingency planning.

## Deliverables
- Change impact assessment
- Readiness notes
- Training and adoption plan
- Rollback considerations
