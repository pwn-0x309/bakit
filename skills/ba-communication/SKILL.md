# BA Communication

Use this skill when the message, audience, and cadence matter as much as the content.

## Workflow
1. Define the audience and objective.
2. Choose the right format for the message.
3. Draft concise updates with decisions, risks, and asks.
4. Set cadence, channels, and escalation path.
5. Capture meeting outcomes and action owners.
6. Keep terminology consistent across documents.

## Deliverables
- Communication plan
- Status report
- Executive summary
- Meeting notes

- Audience and message are aligned
- Actions are explicit
- Updates are short and decision-oriented
