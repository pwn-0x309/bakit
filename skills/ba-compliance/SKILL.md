# BA Compliance

Use this skill when the work has privacy, security, audit, or regulatory implications.

## Workflow
1. Identify the applicable regulations and policies.
2. Classify the data and process sensitivity.
3. Map requirements to obligations and controls.
4. Identify gaps, risks, and evidence needs.
5. Define remediation and validation actions.
6. Document compliance assumptions and open issues.

## Deliverables
- Compliance checklist
- Requirement-to-regulation mapping
- Risk and remediation notes
- Audit evidence needs
