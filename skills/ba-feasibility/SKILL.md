# BA Feasibility

Use this skill when a solution needs a structured viability check before commitment.

## Workflow
1. Define the proposed solution and constraints.
2. Assess technical and operational fit.
3. Evaluate cost, benefit, ROI, and payback.
4. Check schedule realism and dependency risk.
5. Summarize risks, assumptions, and mitigations.
6. Deliver a clear go/no-go recommendation.

## Deliverables
- Feasibility assessment
- Cost-benefit notes
- Risk summary
- Recommendation and contingency options
