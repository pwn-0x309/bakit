# BA Stakeholder

Use this skill when you need to understand who matters, who decides, and how to engage them.

## Workflow
1. List stakeholders, roles, and organizations.
2. Classify influence, interest, and support level.
3. Build a Power/Interest view and RACI where needed.
4. Define communication preferences and cadence.
5. Identify risks, conflicts, and alignment needs.
6. Produce an engagement plan and update path.

## Deliverables
- Stakeholder register
- Power/Interest classification
- RACI matrix
- Communication plan
