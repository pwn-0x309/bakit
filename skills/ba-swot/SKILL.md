# BA SWOT

Use this skill when strategic context or change options need a simple but defensible scan.

## Workflow
1. Define the decision or initiative in scope.
2. List internal strengths and weaknesses.
3. List external opportunities and threats.
4. Convert findings into SO, WO, ST, and WT options.
5. Highlight assumptions and priorities.
6. Summarize the strategic recommendation.

## Deliverables
- SWOT matrix
- Strategy options
- Priority recommendation

## Templates
- Use [swot-template.md](../../templates/swot-template.md)

## Quality Check
- Factors are specific, not generic
- Strategy options follow from the matrix
- Recommendation is traceable to findings
