# BA Workshop

Use this skill when you need to design or run a structured working session.

## Workflow
1. Set the objective, audience, and decision needed.
2. Build an agenda with timing and activities.
3. Prepare prompts, artifacts, and facilitation rules.
4. Capture decisions, actions, and unresolved items.
5. Send follow-up notes and next-step owners.
6. Feed outputs into requirements, stakeholder, or process work.

## Deliverables
- Workshop plan
- Agenda
- Notes and action log
- Follow-up summary

- The workshop has a single primary outcome
- Attendees are appropriate to the decision
- Follow-up actions have owners and dates
